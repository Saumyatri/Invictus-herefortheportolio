Why Donate Blood?

Save a Life

One blood donation can save up to three lives. Donating blood is a selfless act that can have a significant impact on someone in need.

Improve Health

Regular blood donation can improve cardiovascular health and reduce the risk of heart disease. It also helps keep iron levels in the body in check.
